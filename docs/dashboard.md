# 🏛️ ACRIS Dashboard: The Sovereign Guide

The ACRIS platform is designed as a **Role-Based Sovereign Intelligence** environment. Built on the **Sovereign Ledger** design system, the dashboard utilizes high-fidelity visual tokens to communicate regulatory urgency and agentic consensus.

---

## 🛰️ 1. Compliance Officer (The Orchestrator)
*Goal: Manage the ingestion of regulatory data and monitor the autonomous health of the institution.*

### 📄 Policy Analyzer (Sovereign Regulatory Ingress)
- **Why it’s there**: The primary ingress port for the platform. It handles the structural decomposition of internal policies and external circulars.
- **Key Features**: 
    - **Neural Ingest**: A high-action orchestration hub to initiate the AI pipeline.
    - **Institutional Audit Ledger**: A verified history of document indices with neural vector counts.
    - **Live Agent Pulse**: A real-time diagnostic feed showing the logic of autonomous agents (available via the Pipeline Panel).

### 📜 Audit Trail (Immutable Evidence Log)
- **Why it’s there**: To provide immutable record-keeping. Every AI decision and human modification is hashed and timestamped.
- **Key Features**:
    - **Provenance Verification**: Cryptographic signatures for every autonomous action.
    - **Entity Lineage**: Detailed mapping of which agent (e.g., *Refiner-01*) modified which legal paragraph.

### 🧠 Regulatory Q&A (Neural Legal Terminal)
- **Why it’s there**: An "Always-On" regulatory advisory terminal for instant legal clarification.
- **Key Features**: 
    - **Neural Legal Engine**: Real-time analysis of frameworks with specific confidence intervals.
    - **Extracted Obligations**: Automated identification of mandatory secondary requirements from regulatory text.

### 🚨 Early Warning Radar (Neural Threat Horizon)
- **Why it’s there**: Predictive scanning. It identifies high-risk regulatory shifts before they become mandates.
- **Key Features**:
    - **Horizon Scanning Timeline**: A long-term vista of regulatory mandates and enforcement dates.
    - **Radar Insights Overlay**: A persistent floating assistant providing real-time intelligence on radar signals.

## ⚖️ 2. Lawyer (The Remediation Expert)
*Goal: Resolve contradictions, evaluate institutional impact, and institutionalize legal amendments.*

### 📊 Impact Reports (Executive Intelligence Hub)
- **Why it’s there**: Strategic synthesis for executive decision-makers to evaluate the downstream effects of regulatory changes.
- **Key Features**:
    - **Sovereign Synthesis**: High-fidelity archival of neural impact assessments.
    - **Neural Confidence Metrics**: Precision scoring of the AI's analysis for each report.

### 🛠️ Amendment Workbench (Neural Provision Sculptor)
- **Why it’s there**: The **Constitutional Workbench**. This is where contradictions are resolved through precision legal drafting.
- **Key Features**:
    - **Neural Draft Synthesis**: Side-by-side "Proposed vs. Current" legal text with automated rationale generation.
    - **Institutional Lineage**: A version-controlled track of all clause-level modifications before ledger commitment.

### 🔍 Conflict Explorer (Sovereign Ambiguity Lattice)
- **Why it’s there**: To visualize the network of legal contradictions—the **Neural Semantic Web**.
- **Key Features**:
    - **Relational Lattice**: A force-directed graph connecting conflicting legal mandates.
    - **Conflict Provenance**: A step-by-step pipeline audit of how the AI identified semantic misalignment.

### 🏛️ Case Research (Historical Precedent Search)
- **Why it’s there**: To ground current remediation efforts in the context of historical regulatory enforcement and legal precedent.
